## Scripts for making figures in ACACIA future ozone RF paper

NOx_emissions_plots.ipynb: Fig 1 NOx emissions present day and future (SSP1 and SSP3 in 2050)

rfs_present_future_new.ipynb: Fig 2 Present day, SSP1 and SSP3 ozone RF bar charts, absolute and scaled by NOx emissions.

rfs_present_future_burden.ipynb: As above, for ozone burden.

net_nox_future.ipynb: Fig 5 Net NOx ERF bar charts with hatching for SSP1 background.

o3_conc_rf_future.ipynb: Fig 3, Fig 4, Fig S1, Fig S2. Calculation of ozone RFs from ozone mixing ratios. Plots of ozone RF including vertical, zonal distributions and sums. 

UTLS...ipynb: UTLS background NOx, Fig S4.
